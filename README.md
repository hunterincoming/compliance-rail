## Compliance Rail

**Private day-one provenance layer for cannabis genetics**  
Built on Midnight blockchain.

## The Problem
Cannabis breeders and genetics platforms currently have no reliable way to prove they created a new strain on day one. Genetics get renamed, leaked, and stolen. DNA testing and forensic dossiers help after disputes happen — but nothing exists for the exact moment a new cross is created.

## Our Solution
A private, cryptographic timestamp that proves prior existence without ever exposing the raw genetic data. Built as the neutral foundation layer for future licensing, royalty tracking, and chain-of-custody workflows.
